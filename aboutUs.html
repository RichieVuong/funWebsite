About Us

Lucky Shrub, based in Tuscon, Arizona, is a medium-sized garden design firm founded by plant enthusiasts Jason and Maria. Specializing in garden design, landscaping, and maintenance, Lucky Shrub offers expert services to transform outdoor spaces into beautiful oases. Jason leads the design team, while Maria manages marketing and the plant nursery, which provides a diverse selection of indoor and outdoor plants. With a dedication to excellence, Lucky Shrub is your one-stop destination for all your gardening needs.
